About SolarCampers

We are a two man team that have experience building out ford transit connect and sprinter campervans. Our love of the outdoors enticed us to build camper van back in 2016 that we used to travel the country for three weeks. With much positive feedback for our first build we were asked to build camper vans for a few friends. We started Solarcampers to give other adventurers the opportunity to explore and live comfortably on the road.
