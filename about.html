About SolarCampers

We are a two man team that have experience building out Ford Transit Connect and sprinter campervans. Our love of the outdoors enticed us to build a camper van back in 2016 that we used to travel the country for a month. After receiving positive feedback on our first camper van build we were asked to build camper vans for a few friends which prompted us to start Solarcampers. Our hope is to provide other adventurers the opportunity to explore and live comfortably on the road with all the necessities at an affordable cost.
